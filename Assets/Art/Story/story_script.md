# Super Sausage Boy — Intro Story Script

> Original story written for our game. All text below is original work.

## Premise
Super Sausage Boy lives a simple, sizzling life beside Hot Bun Girl. One dark
day, a sinister force — **The Griller** — snatches Hot Bun Girl away across a
gauntlet of spinning saws and salted pits. Now our little banger must run, leap,
and wall-jump through danger to bring her home.

## Narration lines (one per story image/panel)

1. "In a warm little kitchen at the edge of the world lived Super Sausage Boy."
2. "And beside him, glowing golden, was the one he loved most — Hot Bun Girl."
3. "But deep in the dark, something charred and cruel was watching. The Griller."
4. "In a flash of smoke and sparks, Hot Bun Girl was gone — carried off beyond the saws."
5. "Super Sausage Boy did not hesitate. He ran. For her, he would cross any danger."
6. "Run fast. Jump true. Leap from the walls. The gauntlet awaits."

## Tone
Storybook narrator — warm, a little dramatic, building to determination. Voice:
Charon or Oran (deeper, narrator-like).
